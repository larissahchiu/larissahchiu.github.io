Leadership and Involvement.

Society of Women Engineers

The goal of SWE is to foster and empower a community of female engineers. As an Ambassador for SWE, I organize work closely with Officer Council while organizing various social, community outreach, and professional events.

Assosiation of Computer Machinery

ACM's mission is to build of community of computer science students by bringing networking, workshop, mentorship and community events. As a part of ACM^2 (a subgroup of ACM USC), I help organize various CS undergraduate events, such as a undergrad research fair and USC storms VRLA.

Science Outreach

SCout is a community service organizations that aims to inpsire 2nd and 3rd graders in the STEM fields in the surronding LA schools. As a active member of SCout, I teach and mentor a class of 3rd graders in biology, chemistry, and engineering subjects.
